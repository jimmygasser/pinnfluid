ABL inflow conditions used in the dataset
-----------------------------------------
Uref : 5 - 15 m/s   reference wind speed at Zref (varied per case)
Zref : 20 m         reference height (fixed across the dataset)
z0   : 0.1 m        aerodynamic roughness length (fixed across the dataset)

Wind direction is varied per case. Values above were read back from the
per-case meta.json ABL fields across the 302 CFD cases.
